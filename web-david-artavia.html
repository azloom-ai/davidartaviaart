The Artist

David Artavia

"My goal is to create artwork that inspires connection, tells a story, and brings a sense of joy and meaning to the spaces it inhabits."

I am a Costa Rican artist inspired by the country's tropical landscapes, wildlife, and Pacific coastline. Born in San José and largely self-taught, I discovered my passion for art at an early age. Living close to the ocean and surrounded by nature has deeply influenced my work. Through my paintings and murals, I blend realism with imagination to create vibrant, dreamlike scenes that capture the beauty, emotion, and wonder of the world around us.

Custom Commissions

Your Vision, His Brush

David creates custom paintings and murals to order — for your home, business, or as a meaningful gift. The process is collaborative, personal, and transparent from start to finish.

1

Initial Inquiry

Fill out the contact form with a brief description of your project — canvas painting, mural, or custom artwork.

2

Project Details

David responds via email or WhatsApp to gather dimensions, color palette, inspiration images, and any reference photos.

3

Design & Sketch

A concept sketch with up to 3 revisions. If the project moves forward, the fee is credited toward the final price.

$100 Design Fee
4

Agreement & Deposit

A proposal with scope, timeline, and payment schedule. A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking.

50% Deposit
5

Creation & Delivery

The artwork is created to the approved design. The remaining 50% balance is due upon completion, before delivery or final approval.
